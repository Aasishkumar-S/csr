About the Club

Cyber Sonic Rangers

A student-driven cybersecurity club built on curiosity, collaboration, and a relentless drive to push the boundaries of digital intelligence. We forge the next generation of cyber defenders through hands-on events and open learning.

⚔️
Who We Are

Our Identity

Founded by passionate engineers and ethical hackers, Cyber Sonic Rangers is a space where students challenge themselves, share knowledge, and grow into confident digital professionals. From beginners to experts, everyone belongs here.

🎯
What We Do

Our Activities

We run workshops on network security, web exploitation, cryptography, and forensics. We participate in national CTF competitions, organize inter-college events, and mentor students stepping into cybersecurity for the first time.

🌐
Mission & Vision

Why We Exist

Our mission is to make cybersecurity education accessible, exciting, and impactful. Every student deserves the tools to understand digital threats and defend against them. Our vision is a thriving community of ethical hackers equipped not just with skills, but the mindset to use them responsibly — building a safer digital world together.

Cyber Splash 2026

Event Rounds

Four elite rounds. Each one a different dimension of cyber mastery. Every stage is designed to challenge your instincts, sharpen your skills, and separate the prepared from the elite.

Round 01
🧠
Cyber Recon: Knowledge Strike
// Cyber Quiz — Reconnaissance Phase

Every mission begins with reconnaissance. This round puts your theoretical cyber knowledge to the ultimate test — network fundamentals, cryptography, famous exploits, OS internals, and security protocols. Fast-paced, high-pressure, and unforgiving. Like a real recon operative, you have seconds to gather intelligence and act. Only the sharpest minds advance to the breach.


Knowledge Battle · Teams of 2
Round 02
🔐
Password Breach: Crack the Vault
// Password Detection & Solving

The vault is locked. Your job is to break in. Competitors will face a series of encrypted passwords, hashed credentials, and obfuscated ciphers — each one guarding a flag. Using tools, wordlists, and raw cryptographic intuition, you must crack every layer before time expires. This is where pattern recognition meets technical precision. One wrong move costs everything.


Cryptanalysis · Individual
Round 03
🕵️
Operation Dhurandhar: Codebreaker Protocol
// Intelligence Decryption — Elite Cipher Ops

Named after the legendary master of strategy, Operation Dhurandhar is an elite intelligence decryption challenge. Operatives receive intercepted transmissions, encoded messages, and fragmented data streams. Using steganography analysis, cipher breaking, and forensic extraction, they must reconstruct the hidden intelligence. Only agents with sharp minds and sharper instincts will crack the protocol and proceed.


Intel Ops · Individual
Final Round
💻
HackQuest: The Final Breach
// CTF Challenge — Total System Domination

This is the endgame. The surviving elite face a full-spectrum Capture The Flag scenario — a live, multi-layered system with deliberately planted vulnerabilities across web, binary, network, and forensics domains. No hand-holding, no hints. Every flag captured is a battle won in a cyber war that has no mercy. The last team standing with the most flags claimed becomes the undisputed Cyber Splash Champion 2026.
